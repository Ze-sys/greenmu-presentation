Step 3: Problems to Solve

Probing each function to generate problem statements.

Function ID Description Probing Question (PS#) Problem Statement
(PUF) Wind Energy Harvesting PS1: Find a way to enhance the function. How can the overall energy capture of the wind farm be maximized?
PS2: Find a way to resolve a contradiction. To maximize energy capture, turbines must be large and operate at high speeds, but this increases structural stress, cost, and noise.
PS3: Find an alternative way to deliver the function. Are there alternatives to rotational turbines for harvesting wind energy on a large scale (e.g., oscillating foils, kites)?
[PHF] Wind Wake PS4: Find a way to eliminate, reduce or prevent the harmful function. How can the formation of harmful wind wake behind a turbine be prevented or minimized?
PS5: Find a way to benefit from the harmful function. Is there a way to use the energy or characteristics of the wind wake for a useful purpose (e.g., guide airflow)?
[HF2]Slow WindPS4How can the impact of slow wind on downstream turbines be mitigated?
PS5Can the slow wind area be used for a different, beneficial purpose?
[HF3]TurbulencePS4What methods can be used to reduce the turbulence generated by an operating turbine?
PS5Could the turbulent airflow be harnessed or redirected for a positive outcome?
[HF4]Autonomous TurbinePS4How can the negative effects of a turbine operating autonomously (without coordination) be prevented?
PS5Are there scenarios where a turbine operating autonomously is beneficial to the system?
[HF5]Turbine DamagePS4What technologies or strategies can prevent turbine damage from high wind speeds?
PS5Can the detection of conditions that cause turbine damage be used to trigger a useful secondary function?
(UF1)Turbine RotationPS1How can turbine rotation be made more efficient at various wind speeds?
PS2Turbine must rotate to generate power, but rotation itself causes harmful turbulence and wake.
PS3What are alternative ways to capture kinetic energy from wind without large rotating blades?
(UF2)Turbine Op. CoordinationPS1How can the coordination between turbines be optimized in real-time for maximum farm efficiency?
PS2Coordination is required to manage the farm, but each turbine operating autonomously seeks to maximize its own power, creating a conflict.
PS3Are there decentralized or passive methods to achieve farm-level coordination without active control?
(UF3)High Wind SpeedPS1How can the system better utilize periods of high wind speed safely?
PS2High wind speed is good for power generation but it causes turbine damage.
PS3Can we store kinetic energy from gusts without over-stressing the turbine?
(UF4)Wind Speed & DirectionPS1How can the measurement of wind speed/direction be more accurate and predictive?
PS2To accurately measure wind, sensors must be upstream, but this can create minor turbulence.
PS3Can we use remote sensing (LiDAR) or airflow models instead of local anemometers?
(UF5)Turbine ManagementPS1How can the management system react faster to changing conditions?
PS2Centralized management offers optimal control but creates a single point of failure.
PS3Can turbines use swarm intelligence to self-organize and manage the farm?
(UF6)Control Blade YawPS1How can yaw control be more precise and energy-efficient?
PS2Constantly adjusting yaw maximizes capture but consumes energy and causes wear.
PS3Are there blade designs less sensitive to wind direction, reducing the need for yaw?

PS3Are there direct-drive systems that offer better performance?

PS3Can direct-drive generators eliminate the need for a gearbox entirely?
