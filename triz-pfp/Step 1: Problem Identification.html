Step 1: Problem Identification

Defining the core purpose and the primary problem of the system.

Wind Energy Harvesting System

  • System Description: A wind farm with multiple turbines designed to capture kinetic energy from the wind and convert it into electrical energy.
  • Customer Need: A reliable, efficient, and scalable system for generating clean energy from wind, while maximizing power output and minimizing operational disruptions.
  • Critical Challenge: The operation of an upstream turbine creates turbulent, lower-energy air flow (a "wind wake"), which negatively impacts the performance and lifespan of downstream turbines.

Primary Useful Function (PUF)

The main intended purpose of the system.

(PUF) Wind Energy Harvesting

Primary Harmful Function (PHF)

The main undesirable outcome or problem.

[PHF] Wind Wake
